Our story

Rooted in Khorfakkan since 2018

Al Yasmeen Al Abyad — "the white jasmine" — has been arranging flowers for the Khorfakkan community since 2018. What started as a small shop on Al Lulouyya has grown into a trusted name for weddings, gifting, and everyday bouquets.

Every order, fresh or artificial, is arranged by hand and wrapped with the same care whether it's a single stem or a full event.
